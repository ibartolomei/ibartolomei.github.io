I love my dog, and here's why you should too.

She's a good girl. Her shiny chocolate fur coat resembles that of a seal's. She bounds along fields and exudes giddy energy everywhere she goes. She's a COVID puppy, so she's a little weird, but her quirkiness never stopped her from gaining attention. Instead of wagging her tail, she swings it like a helicopter. Acting like she doesn't have 3 brothers and a dad, her bark doesn't know the difference between an innocent male bystander and a threat to humanity. A sibling comes home from college? Piss on the floor! She is our hero and the one we love the most, and if you could ever meet her, you'd think the same.

Labs are an excellent family dog. She made quarantine much easier by just being herself and bringing energy back into the house. Here are some facts about labs if you ever think about investing in the best breed in the world:

  • Endless energy
  • Recommended to put them in obedience classes
  • Versatile workers and hunter blood!
  • Color does not indicate any particular character type
  • They love to swim
If you're ever considering getting a labrador, remember these facts! In any case, however, you'll love your lab. Nova is one great example of so many.
